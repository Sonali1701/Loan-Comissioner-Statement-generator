Payroll Instructions (MVP)

  1. Enter the required fields (Loan Officer Name, Total Check Amount, Pay Date).
  2. Enter reimbursements included in the check (these reduce commissionable amount).
  3. Optionally enter a split percentage (e.g. 80 for 80%).
  4. Enter deductions and additions as needed.
  5. Review the calculated outputs.
  6. Click Generate Statement (PDF) to download a statement.

Tip: You can also click Print to print the entry page (not the PDF).
